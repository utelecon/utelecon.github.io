Troubleshooting guide: When the error message “Unable to update the session management database” is displayed, and the download page is not displayed There is a possibility that you have not completed the Information Security Training course. If that is the case, you cannot use UTokyo VPN. Please contact the Security Team of the Information Environment Division at the headquarters, jouhousecurity.adm __at__ gs.mail.u-tokyo.ac.jp (change __at__ to @), with your UTokyo Account user name.
If the issue is still not resolved, please consult the Technical Support Desk.
