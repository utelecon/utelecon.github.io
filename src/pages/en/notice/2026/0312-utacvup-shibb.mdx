---
title: Sign-in with UTokyo Account is temporarily unavailable on some systems due to maintenance (March 12, 2026, 7:00 PM)
---

Thank you for using UTokyo Account. We will be performing a version upgrade of the server responsible for sign-in integration within the UTokyo Account authentication infrastructure. As a result, signing in with a UTokyo Account will be temporarily unavailable as described below. We appreciate your understanding and cooperation.

## Date and Time

Thursday, March 12, 2026, from 19:00 to 20:00 (for a maximum of approximately 15 minutes during this window).

In addition to this page, any updates regarding the status on the day or changes to the schedule will be announced on the [System Failures and Maintenance Information](https://univtokyo.sharepoint.com/sites/utokyoaccount/SitePages/en/service-status.aspx) page.

## Impact

* During the maintenance, you may be unable to sign in to the systems listed below for the first time (the sign-in screen may not be displayed).
* You may be required to sign in again after service is restored. There are no changes to your account.
