This method allows you to verify your identity using passkeys saved in services such as "iCloud Keychain" or "Google Password Manager". Use this as your second or subsequent verification method after registering another verification method. Note that identity verification using passkeys is currently a preview feature, so the assistance the support desk can provide may be limited. For details, refer to [Using Passkeys for UTokyo Account Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)](/utokyo_account/mfa/passkey/).
