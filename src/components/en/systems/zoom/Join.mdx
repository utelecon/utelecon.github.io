1. In the meeting room, your microphone audio or camera image may be sent to other people (in the test room used here, your audio or video will not be sent to others just by joining the room. However, it could be sent while testing different functions). If you are concerned, please perform this test in a situation that is safe for sending audio and video.
2. Access the test meeting room URL via the [ITC-LMS test cource](https://itc-lms.ecc.u-tokyo.ac.jp/lms/course?idnumber=20197J919010V02).
3. You may be asked to click a confirmation button or select an application to open.
4. After the Zoom app opens, when you see the message "Connect to audio to hear other users", click "Call using the Internet".
5. If you get a black screen with "Start Video" and "Participants" at the bottom, you are in the meeting room.
